Food Ordering App (2022)

Overview

This app was created as part of a series of labs in the course MSCI 343: Human-Computer Interaction. The labs were for learning how to use Figma and to apply UX knowledge. I was given the freedom to design any app, and I designed a simple food ordering app.

My Contribution and Takeaways

In creating this app, I learned how to create and design screens, buttons, dropdowns, and more. The video above shows a little bit of the design. The app was further improved by adding a checkout page, and improved ordering options as shown below.

Possibilities of Further Development

I'd like to learn more about Figma. I would like to create more complex controls and be able to prototype complex apps and controls.
