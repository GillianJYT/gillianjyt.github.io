Card Game (2022)

Overview

I designed and developed this game for my Teaching Assistant position at the University of Waterloo. The game uses various aspects of Excel VBA and controls and it was my students' final project to recreate the game.

My Contribution and Takeaways

Based off of the learning outcomes outlined by the professor, I brainstormed and designed a game. In coding the game, I adjusted and added to the game to include various aspects of Excel that I have taught throughout the term. It includes buttons, pop-up windows, autopopulating cells, calculation functions, and more.
