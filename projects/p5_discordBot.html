Discord Bot (2021-2022)

Overview

Being stuck at home during COVID-19, Discord became my place to hang out and chat with friends. One of our activities was finding random Discord bots and playing with their various commands; some were Pokemon themed, some had card games, and others were linked to video games. I wanted to create my own and add in fun commands for my friends to try out.
