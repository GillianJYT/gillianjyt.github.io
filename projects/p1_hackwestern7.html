Hack Western 7 (Nov 2020)

You're talking a long walk and feel like sitting down. Where's the nearest bench? Or you finish your coffee, but don't see a disposal bin nearby. What do you do?

Bench Spot App

The app is a navigation app based in Toronto that you can use on your walks to help find and navigate to nearby benches, public washrooms, garbage and recycling bins, and more!

How It Works

Bench Spot uses your phoneâ€™s current location, the latitude and longitude data of all city furniture and calculates the distances to find and display the ones nearest to you!

Technologies Used

We used Java and XML for the backend, built the app in Android Studio with map integration through Google Maps API, and collected our data from the City of Toronto Open Data Set.

My Contribution and Takeaways

I worked on the front-end of the appliation. This involved working in Android Studio, and designing and assessing UX. I learned a lot about Android Studio and XML, which helps me in any future mobile app development for Android devices.

Possibilities of Further Development

We could deploy the app using Google Cloud for public use.

Given alternate data, this app could be converted to show the nearest of any type of object. For example, it could show the nearest restroom for travellers, or the nearest dog park for someone who just moved in.
